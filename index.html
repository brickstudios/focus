Because I don’t understand the business problem of a product I dislike I choose to focus on a more personal app - the ‘home’ screen. Remember the HS? That little house icon? Netscape. It still exists. but our behavior around it has changed. So I chose to create a custom Google Chrome Extension. One that would replace my current one – Momentum. Don’t get me wrong it’s nice, but it’s worst feature is the lack of customization. If it were more modular to customize in a minimal direction (ex: only 5-6 items on the scene) I would find it more useful. Right now it’s sort of informative & pretty (photos). But what if it could be both?

Side bar: my wife & I have our photo streams integrated into all of the screens savers in the house particularly the TV screens. It’s been a delight to have recent memories played back to us regularly & passively. particularly my Instagram pics. I wanted this extension to have the same effect. A reflection of the recent past (instagram), temperature and clock for the present and a limited 2 week calendar for the future.

The presentation has to be minimal, direct and weighted accordingly.

I call it FOCUS.
